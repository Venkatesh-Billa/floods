Flood Warning Issued

Breaking News!

Emergency Alert: Immediate Action Required

A severe flood warning has been issued for coastal and low-lying areas. Please take immediate precautions and follow the instructions below.

What You Need to Do

  • Evacuate or Stay Indoors: Evacuate immediately if instructed. If not, stay indoors and avoid unnecessary travel.
  • Avoid Flooded Areas: Do not attempt to walk or drive through flooded roads. It can be life-threatening.
  • Secure Your Home: Move valuables to higher ground, and shut off electricity and gas if necessary.
  • Stay Informed: Tune into local news and weather channels for real-time updates.

Emergency Kit Checklist

  • Water & Non-perishable food (3-day supply)
  • First Aid Kit & Medications
  • Flashlight & Extra Batteries
  • Important Documents in a waterproof container
  • Clothing, Blankets, and Charged Phone
